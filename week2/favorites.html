Some of my favorite things

By Ethan Hamilton

Favorite Movie

My favorite movie is Nightcrawler. This movie is a thriller about a petty criminal who starts to make a name for himself as a cameraman recording footage of accidents or crime scenes, and works his way up in the local news media hierarchy.

Favorite Music

My favorite musician is a French composer by the name of Maurice Ravel. He is considered to have been a part of the early 20th century impressionist movement, although he disliked the use of that term for his music. Here is his Wikipedia page.
